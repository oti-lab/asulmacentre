About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence Since 2009

Our History

Founded in 2009, Modern Academy has been a beacon of educational excellence for over two decades. Our institution has grown from a small school to a leading educational center, serving students from diverse backgrounds and empowering them to achieve their dreams.

Through constant innovation in teaching methods, investment in modern facilities, and a commitment to holistic development, we have maintained our position as a premier educational institution.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a world-class education that develops critical thinkers, fosters creativity, and instills values of integrity and responsibility in every student.

Our Vision

To create a dynamic learning environment where students excel academically, develop character, and prepare for meaningful contributions to society.

School Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do, from academics to High school life

Integrity

Honesty and ethical behavior form the foundation of our school community.

Inclusion

We celebrate diversity and ensure every student feels valued and supported.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas and technologies to enhance the learning experience.

Admissions

Join Our Community of Learners

Application Process

  1. Step 1: Complete the online application form on our portal
  2. Step 2: Submit required documents (transcripts, recommendations, test scores)
  3. Step 3: Attend a personal interview with the admissions team
  4. Step 4: Receive admission decision within 2 weeks
  5. Step 5: Complete enrollment and orientation

Admission Requirements

Academic Credentials

• Previous school transcripts
• Minimum GPA requirements (varies by grade)
• Standardized test scores

Documentation

• Birth certificate
• Medical records
• Two teacher recommendations

Other Requirements

• Personal statement
• Parent/guardian information
• Residency proof
